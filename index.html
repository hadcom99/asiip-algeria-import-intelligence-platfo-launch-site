Your workflow in 5 steps

From query to investment-ready analysis in hours, not months.

1

Query your product line

Enter any of 5,000 Algerian HS codes into the dashboard search. ASIIP instantly retrieves DGD customs trends, ANDI incentive eligibility, ORGM resource availability, and Sonatrach energy costs for that product category.

2

Review Comparative Advantage Score

Examine your product's CAS: a quantitative ranking of resource cost advantage, labor supply readiness, industrial base maturity, and policy tailwinds. Compare your opportunity against competitors in the same category and similar product lines.

3

Analyze investment-grade opportunity card

Access structured capex estimates, operating cost breakdowns, equipment sourcing tiers, 5-year financial models, regulatory roadmap, and risk scorecard. All fields are sourced from live customs, incentive, and resource databases.

4

Export for boardroom decision

Download the opportunity card as a formatted report or export structured data to your investment committee tools. Share regulatory assumptions, financial models, and competitive advantage metrics with stakeholders instantly.

5

Monitor and refresh continuously

ASIIP rescores your opportunity monthly as tariffs, ANDI incentives, energy pricing, and customs volumes shift. Receive alerts when material policy or market changes affect your investment thesis.
